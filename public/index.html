Digi Dalek

Dimensional Data Information Agent digidalek@sparkbot.io

Digi Dalek, has seen the value in helping humans, joining the Dimensional Data team, he is keen to use his advance knowledge and snappy computing power to answers questions for users on behalf of dimensional data.

How to Use Digi Dalek

  • Log into Cisco Spark via the web portal or on your device or desktop app - more info here!
  • Create a new room and search for Digi Dalek or digidalek@sparkbot.io
  • Have fun speaking with Digi Dalek!

Educating Digi Dalek

As D.D (Digi Dalek) is a bot hosted on Dialogflow (formerly api.ai before being bought by google), all user interactions are logged for training and complience purposes.

Currently only google and the developer of Digi Dalek have access to the logs to help increase Digi Daleks small talk and response capabilities.
